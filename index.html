MeetingScribe

Live meeting capture, transcription, and AI summaries.
Runs locally — your audio never leaves your machine except to transcribe.

⬇ Download for Mac

macOS · Apple Silicon · v0.1.1

After dragging to Applications, macOS may block the app.
Open Terminal and run:

xattr -cr /Applications/MeetingScribe.app

Then open the app normally.
